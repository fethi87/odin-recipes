Broccoli Caesar Salad

Caesar Salad Dressing is popular. You may have a bottle of it in your fridge. This is a simple salad of Broccoli dressed with the dressing. I recommend to add Nuts such as Almonds to this salad. You can use uncooked Broccoli, but I prefer Broccoli slightly cooked, only slightly.

Ingredients

  • 1 head Broccoli *cut into small florets, thick stem sliced
  • Caesar Salad 2 to 3 tablespoons Almonds *Today I used Almond Flakes
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast Almonds (OR other nuts of your choice) until lightly browned, then coarsely chop. Today I used Almond Flakes.
  2. Make Caesar Dressing. Lightly season it with Salt & Black Pepper.
  3. .Make Caesar Dressing. Lightly season it with Salt & Black Pepper.
  4. Combine Broccoli, Caesar Dressing, Almonds and Parmesan. Add extra freshly ground Black Pepper and serve.
